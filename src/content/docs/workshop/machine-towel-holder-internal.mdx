## Premise
Create a paper towel holder out of custom cut wood for the base and a wrought iron pipe as the rod. End users will design and cut their own base plate and then drill hole for the pipe to press fit in. After that they will stain wood and have a custom paper towel holder

## Logistics
Capacity: 6-7

Volunteers requested: maybe 1 or two

Location: 1st floor, machine shop
